About Us

The Story of Mr. Boot

Founded in 2009 by Pankaj Suresh Sonawane, Mr. Boot Shoe Laundry and Repairing Services has been Pimpri-Chinchwad's most trusted shoe care destination for over 15 years.

What started as a small repair shop near DY Patil has grown into a comprehensive care studio — shoes, jackets, bags — all under one trusted roof.

Our Journey

15+ Years of Excellence

2009
Founded by Pankaj Suresh Sonawane in Sant Tukaram Nagar. Started as a small shoe repair workshop near DY Patil.
2012
Expanded to include professional shoe laundry and deep cleaning services for all footwear types.
2016
Introduced custom bespoke shoe making — one of the first in the Pimpri-Chinchwad area.
2020
Launched WhatsApp ordering. Added jacket and bag care services during pandemic recovery.

Achieved 4.7★ Google rating with 50+ verified reviews. Top-rated shop in Pimpri-Chinchwad.
